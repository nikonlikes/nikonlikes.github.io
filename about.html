About World Cup Kits Shop

Our Mission

World Cup Kits Shop is a demonstration project showcasing a static e-commerce website built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Our goal is to provide a clean, accessible, and performant shopping experience for football fans looking to own a piece of World Cup history.

Features

  • Responsive design that works on all devices
  • WCAG-AA compliant accessibility
  • No build step required - pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Local storage-based shopping cart
  • Filter kits by team and year
  • Dark mode support

Important Disclaimer

This is a demonstration project and is not officially associated with FIFA or any football governing body. All team names, designs, and World Cup references are used for educational purposes only. No actual merchandise is being sold through this website.

Contact

For questions about this demonstration project, please visit our GitHub repository .

Privacy

This demo site uses local storage to maintain your shopping cart state. No data is sent to any server. All data is stored locally in your browser and can be cleared at any time by using the "Empty Cart" button or clearing your browser's local storage.
